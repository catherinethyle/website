"this is love" Artist Statement

This participatory art piece called for viewers to take a crayon and follow this prompt: "write or draw (it doesn’t matter how well) whatever comes to mind. it can be the name of a sushi place you love, your favorite brand, the name of your best friend or parent, you and your partner’s anniversary, a song that you have been obsessed with recently, a letter/note to someone you love, the list is infinitely long". this piece explored what other people love in their loves.

Bio

Catherine Thy Lê is a first generation, Vietnamese American interdisciplinary artist who was born and raised in Fontana, Southern California. She is currently pursuing her BFA in Digital Media Art as well as a minor in sculpture at San Jose State University. Catherine has been working as an art handler and gallery installer at the Natalie and James Thompson Gallery at San Jose State University and hopes to pursue a career in art installation and fabrication on top of expanding her own professional career and practice in the art field.

-Bio-

Catherine Thy Lê is a first generation Vietnamese American interdisciplinary artist who was born and raised in Fontana, Southern California. She is currently pursuing her Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) with a concentration in Digital Media Art, as well as a minor in sculpture at San Jose State University.

Catherine has been working as an art handler and gallery installer at the Natalie and James Thompson Gallery at San Jose State University and hopes to pursue a career in art installation and fabrication. She also hopes expanding her own professional career and practice in the art field.

Catherine plans to obtain her Masters of Fine Arts (MFA) in the future. She is working towards opening a coffee shop in the future that bridges her love for coffee and art. The space would provide community members with a place where they can explore their creativity by providing free materials and exhibiting artwork of local artists.
